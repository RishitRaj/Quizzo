About Quizzo

Quizzo is an exciting online platform that brings the fun of quizzing right to your screen. Whether you're a trivia lover, a curious learner, or just someone looking for a quick mental challenge, Quizzo has something for you. With a wide range of topics and carefully crafted multiple-choice questions, Quizzo offers a fun and competitive experience. The goal is simple — test your knowledge, aim for the highest score, and enjoy the thrill of learning along the way. Ready to jump into the world of quizzes? Click start and let the challenge begin. Fasten your seatbelt — it’s going to be a mind-tickling ride!

Instructions

To begin your quiz journey, simply click the Start button below. You’ll first be asked to choose a topic from a list of exciting categories. Once selected, the quiz will begin immediately. Each quiz contains 10 multiple-choice questions, and you’ll have 5 minutes to complete them. A countdown timer will appear on your screen, and the quiz will automatically submit when the timer runs out — so make sure to keep an eye on it! Questions will be shown one at a time, allowing you to focus on each without distractions. After finishing, your score will be displayed on the results page. Choose wisely, think quickly, and most importantly — have fun!
